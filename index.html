Sound Familiar?

Structured enough to work. Relaxed enough to enjoy.

Some networking groups ask for more time than your business can spare. Others are so loose that meetings wander and you leave wondering why you came. If you've tried both, you know how hard the middle is to find.

That middle is what we've built: a consistent weekly agenda that keeps referrals moving, without the rigid rules that turn networking into a second job.

Who We Are

Referrals. Support. Camaraderie.

We're a network of entrepreneurs, sales representatives, and professionals who meet weekly to widen each other's circles, sharpen each other's skills, and celebrate each other's wins. Most of us run smaller companies, so we know how much every relationship counts.

Our members serve both businesses and households: mortgages and home buying, personal and commercial insurance, financial planning for owners and families, marketing, technology, and strategy. That means the referrals you give and receive here can come from anywhere in your life, not just your business contacts.

How It Works

What a Monday morning with us looks like

One hour, a clear agenda, and a room that wants you to win.

Open networking
